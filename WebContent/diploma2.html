Overview

The curriculum of the Diploma in Creative Writing in English program enables one to further nurture one’s aptitude for creative writing by polishing one’s writing skills and honing one’s potential to be a professional writer. It can help one to creatively write in different genres / forms, including poetry,screen writing and memoir. If one is interested in writing, there are a number of related fields that one may join, such as journalism, communication and media, advertising and public relations, corporate communications, professional writing and rhetoric, publishing etc.

The program will help the learners to Get an exposure to a wide range of experiences expressed in the different forms of writing.Get introduced to the lives and views of well-known writers and media personalities. Through these, learners will get inspiration and guidance. Stimulate their creative thinking and writing.Dwell in the realm of imagination and bring out the fascinating insights gained through writing.

Duration

The duration of the Diploma in Creative Writing in Englishis 1 year. The registration of the participant is valid for two years.

Eligibility

Standard 12th pass-out with minimum 50% in English, OR Diploma Holder with Standard 12th Qualification with Minimum 50% in English, OR Graduate with minimum 50% in English at graduation level, OR International/SAARC students who have graduated from a recognized University/Institution with minimum 50% in English at Graduation Level.

NOTE:Students enrolling for Creative Writing program should have passed 12th grade from an English medium school.
